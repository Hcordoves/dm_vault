The World

The Forgotten Realms in 1508 DR is a world under pressure. The civilization of the North — long protected by the Lord's Alliance — is being systematically threatened by the Spree Alliance, a shadow organization that wants to tear down the gods and remake the world in their image. The fey, displaced from their home plane, have carved out territory in Silverymoon. Old heroes are wounded or missing. The party stands in the gap.

Party Notes

Campaign So Far

The party has been operating as independent adventurers in the North during a period of mounting crisis. The Spree Alliance has been attacking Lord's Alliance cities — Yartar was assaulted and liberated with the party's help. A Tomb of the Unnamed Heroes was built northeast of Yartar in memory of those who fell.

Silverymoon — the Gem of the North — was hit hardest. During the Spree's invasion, the great champion Mourn Eveningfall fought the god Bane directly. The battle destroyed Silverymoon's magical wards. The city was left exposed and is now under Fey Alliance control. Mourn survived, but his current whereabouts are unknown.

The party's current objective: find Oberon's champion, who has gone missing somewhere in the High Forest.
